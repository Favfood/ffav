Pasta Salad





Ingredients


  • 2 tsp finely chopped red onion
  • 1 Â½ tsp of caper
  • 1 tbsp of pesto
  • 2 tsp of olive oil
  • 1 can of spring water tuna, drained
  • 100g of pasta (we reccomend fusilli)
    •

    Method




    1. Mix the capers, onion, oil and pesto then set aside
    2. Boil a pot of water and then add the pasta with a pinch of salt,
      making sure to stir every now and then
      cook until al dente
    3. Drain the pasta then rinse under cold water
    4. Flake the tuna into a bowl with the pasta,
      then mix in the pesto mixture
